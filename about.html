About.

Welcome! We're all about making your bath time a little more special — a moment to refresh yourself.

Each bath sponge we offer has a cool story, from the calm seas to the earth's treasures, and the talented people who turn raw materials into something nice for your skin.

We're here to celebrate that story, bringing balance, beauty, and eco-friendly options back into your daily life through basic, natural bath stuff.

🌿 How We Got Started

Our story began with a question:
Why did we turn a simple pleasure into something we just throw away?

Way back when, things like natural sponges were used everywhere. They were taken with care, used with intention, and valued for what they were. But then came plastic stuff and big factories, and those old tools got replaced. Sure, those things cleaned the skin, but they weren't so great for the planet.

So, we wanted to bring back what was lost.
Our story didn't start in some factory; it started in nature. We saw how sea sponges grow nicely in the ocean, how a loofah turns into something that gently scrubs your skin, and how a konjac root can make super-soft sponges.

These materials don't just clean; they connect us. They remind us of the earth and those old ways that valued less stuff.

That's why we started, to make bath sponges that are all about tradition, nature, and making modern life a little better.

💧 Our Goal

We think real beauty starts with care — caring for yourself, others, and the world.
Our goal is to sell bath stuff that makes taking care of yourself every day cleaner, gentler, and better for the earth.

We don't follow trends or try to make a ton of stuff. We focus on making good-quality, responsible products. Everything we sell is picked because it works well, lasts long, and is good.

We have three rules we follow:

For Your Body — making sponges that feel good, clean well, and are good for all skin types.

For the Planet — using materials that break down naturally and are kind to the earth.

For You — inspiring people to relax and enjoy taking care of themselves.

To us, a bath sponge is more than just a tool. It shows how nature and our daily habits are linked.

🌸 Our Way of Thinking

We believe that doing small things can make a big difference.
Taking a few moments each day to care for yourself can make you feel better, both inside and out.

That's why we value these things:

Keep It Simple: We stick to the basics. Our products do what they need to do, and that's it. No extra stuff, just good, honest materials.

Be Aware: We think taking care of your skin is like being aware of the moment. The way a sponge feels, the smell of soap — it all brings you back to the present.

Be Respectful: We respect the natural world that gives us these products. Our sponges are taken, made, and packaged with respect for people and the planet.

We hope this way of thinking brings a bit of calm and peace to every bathroom.

🌊 What We Make

Each sponge we offer shows where it comes from. Every material has its own story.

Natural Sea Sponges

Taken from the ocean carefully, these sponges are soft and soak up water well. They also have stuff in them that fights off germs, making them a nice thing to use in the bath. They make a rich, creamy lather and give you a gentle massage, leaving your skin clean and refreshed.

Plant-Based Loofahs

Loofahs come from a plant like a cucumber. When they dry, they're great for scrubbing your skin. Ours are grown naturally, dried in the sun, and not changed too much, so they stay pure. They're great for getting rid of dead skin and making your skin smooth and glowy!

Konjac Sponges

Made from the root of the konjac plant, these sponges are extra gentle. They're awesome for taking care of your face and for sensitive skin. They're soft and clean without making your skin irritated.

Good Synthetic Sponges

If you want something long-lasting, we also have synthetic sponges that are made from safe materials. They're light, easy to clean, and make a good lather with just a bit of soap.

Every sponge is good for something. Together, they show that nature gives us cool tools for taking care of ourselves — if we just use them right.

🌱 Our Promise to the Earth

Being good to the earth is super important to us.
We know that what we do today affects tomorrow. That's why we hold ourselves responsible for everything.

Taking Good Care: Our sea sponges are gathered carefully, so they can grow back naturally, which keeps the oceans healthy.

Good Plants: Our loofahs and konjac sponges come from plants that grow fast and don't need much to grow.

Good Packaging: We try to use packaging that can be recycled, and we don't use much plastic.

Good Friends: We work with people who also care about treating people fairly and being good to the environment.

We think being good to the earth isn't just something to say; it's something to do.
